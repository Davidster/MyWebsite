Myostro

Description

Myostro is a project that I built at a MHacks 6 with a team of three other students from McGill University. Our goal was to use the Myo Armband to empower DJs, allowing them to use gestures with their arm & hand in order to orchestrate a musical spectacle.

The application allowed for the selection of tracks through pointing one's arm in the direction of the desired track. Once a track was selected, the DJ could perform a fist gesture to toggle it between playing and not playing. The volume of each track could also be controlled by rotating one's hand in either direction.
